My Journey

I was born in 1993 and raised in Hörden, a tiny village in the centre of Germany. As there was not too much stuff to do there, I developed an interest in technology and coding fairly early on. After finishing high school, I decided to study information systems at the university of Münster which offered an excellent program combining elements of computer science and buissnes administration.

After completing my bachelor's degree, I was a bit indecisive whether to switch to computer science or not. However, I decided to pick a rather unusual combination of subjects reaching deep into computer science on the hand and getting exposure to advanced finance topics on the other hand allowing me to go for a master's degree in information system. As part of my master's study, I got the opportunity to study abroad in the small, yet buitiful Kristiansand, Norway and the buzzling metropolis Madrid, Spain.

I graduated from university in 2019 and decided to join Deutsche Bank, first by completing its technology trainee programm and than at associate engineer. Since than, I improved my software development skills significantly, gained experience working in a very international environment and contributed to a rather diverse set of applications.
